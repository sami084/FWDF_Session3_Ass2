About Acadgild

Acadgild team believe that software development requires highly specialized skills, best learned with guidance from experienced
practitioners. Online videos or classroom formats are poor substitutes for building real projects with the help of a dedicated
mentor.

Acadgild's mission is to bring accessible, affordable, engaging, and highly effective quality education to the world. We believe
that higher education is a basic human right, and we seek to empower our students to advance their education and careers.
